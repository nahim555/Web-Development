Accomodation Info

Equipped with a conservatory leading to an enclosed garden, enjoy amazing sea views from both the ground and first floor bedrooms.

This mid-terrace chalet is located on the outskirts of the village of Burgh Castle in Norfolk. This lovely chalet is set on the edge of Kingfisher Holiday Park, overlooking the grounds and fields to the marina beyond. From the small patio outside you step straight into the main living area, which is open plan with a small, well-equipped kitchen with dining area and comfortable sofas to sink into after a day out exploring. One of the sofas can be used as a sofa bed, offering greater sleeping flexibility. Directly off the main living area are two bedrooms and a family shower room with a large walk-in shower. Kingfisher Holiday Park has beautifully presented well-kept lawns, ideal for children to enjoy the wide open spaces. You can walk for miles directly from your doorstep along the riverbank enjoying beautiful views over the marshes and the area is a real haven for wildlife. Why not take the footpath to the marina where you can stop at the pub for a bite to eat before returning home to your holiday chalet. With Great Yarmouth on your doorstep, the Norfolk Broads and the wonderful Norfolk Coast just 10 minutes' away, this holiday chalet is in an ideal location to explore this wonderful part of the country
